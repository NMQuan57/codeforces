The problem can be addressed as follow:
    1. The cursor need to be clicked at least four times to complete a full sequence.
    2. The cursor moving distances between numbers is the difference between two numbers.
    3. Therefore, we can calculate the number of moves easily by calculating the differences between two adjacent numbers, note that the first numbers will be compared with 1 as there is no number before it.
    4. Because 0 is located at the end of the sequence, we cannot calculate the difference normally, thus, we need to replace 0 with 10 for easier calculation.
